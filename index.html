The online version of the popular classic boardgame - Mastermind!

Click on the color to place the peg

Game rules

The computer has selected a 4-color pattern and you have to guess the pattern within 10 guesses.

Click the color pegs to add your combination and click "Check guess" button and the machine will use black and white score pegs to give you clues on the entered combination.

Each white peg indicates that one of your pegs is in the right position.
Each black peg indicates that one of your pegs is the right color AND the right position.
4 black pegs indicate that you have won!

Use the clues to guess the correct combination within 10 tries.
